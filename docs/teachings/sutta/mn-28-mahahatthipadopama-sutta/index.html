[Analysis of Sensory Consciousness]

‚ÄúVenerables, just as when space is encompassed by wood, rope, thatch, and clay it is called a ‚Äòhouse,‚Äô in the same way, when space is encompassed by bones, sinews, muscle, and skin it is called a ‚Äòbody.‚Äô
